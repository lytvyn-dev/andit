Digital signature & mail encryption

Digital signatures guarantee the validity and authenticity of a digital document. Each document receives a special encrypted digital authentication. When you apply the electronic encrypted authentication of a digital signature to e-mails, macros, or other documents, you guarantee that the document really comes from the signer and has not been modified. Therefore, you not only protect your digital documents, but also make it easier to quickly send and work with your confidential documents.
